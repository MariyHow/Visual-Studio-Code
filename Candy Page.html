Soft Candy

Soft candy has a texture that is easy to chew and just melts in the mouth while eating. Examples of soft candies are toffee, fudge, chewy types of candy, etc. It is impossible to talk about soft candy and not mention Jelly Beans, Sour Patch Kids, and ourfavorite marshmallows.
